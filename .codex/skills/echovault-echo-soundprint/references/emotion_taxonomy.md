# EchoVault emotion taxonomy

## Core dimensions

Score every echo on these dimensions from 0.0 to 1.0 unless noted otherwise:

- `valence`: painful/dark to joyful/light.
- `energy`: still/quiet to kinetic/electric.
- `intensity`: background feeling to overwhelming feeling.
- `clarity`: foggy/ambiguous to clearly understood.
- `motion`: stuck/looping to moving/transforming.
- `socialness`: solitary/internal to shared/relational.
- `nostalgia`: present-tense to memory-soaked.
- `tension`: resolved/safe to unresolved/frictive.

## Primary mood families

| Family | Emotional role | Soundprint tendency | Orb/echo visual |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| grief | hold loss without rushing it | slow tempo, minor mode, sparse vocals, piano/ambient/indie | blue-violet orb, rain grain, soft blur |
| ache | romantic, lonely, or longing | mid-slow, warm pads, intimate vocals, reverb | rose-blue glow, glass reflections |
| calm | regulate and breathe | low energy, acoustic/ambient, low percussion | pale aqua orb, wide negative space |
| joy | amplify lightness | major mode, bright tempo, pop/funk/disco | gold orb, lens flare, upward motion |
| awe | make the moment feel mythic | cinematic builds, strings, synth swell | silver-gold orb, cosmic scale |
| anger | channel heat without glamorizing harm | high energy, percussion, rock/rap/electronic edge | red-orange orb, sharp contrast |
| confidence | main-character momentum | bold beat, anthemic hook, clean bass | emerald/gold orb, spotlight framing |
| focus | sharpen attention | steady beat, minimal lyrics, electronic/lofi | white-blue orb, grid/constellation lines |
| nostalgia | archive the memory | older textures, warm analog, soft crackle | amber orb, film grain, sunset haze |
| hope | gentle upward movement | rising chord progressions, mid tempo, warm vocals | green-gold orb, dawn light |
| numb | make emptiness legible | minimal, drone, muted, low melodic motion | gray-blue orb, fog, low opacity |
| chaos | reflect overload but create structure | complex rhythm, glitch, dark synth, controlled intensity | fractured neon orb, motion blur |

## Secondary modifiers

Add modifiers to avoid one-note recommendations:

- `cinematic`: orchestral, soundtrack, expansive mixes.
- `intimate`: close vocals, bedroom pop, acoustic, low loudness.
- `nocturnal`: dark synths, late-night R&B, trip-hop, low brightness.
- `sunlit`: bright pop, disco, indie, higher valence.
- `stormy`: distorted guitars, heavy percussion, minor mode.
- `weightless`: ambient, dream pop, reverb-heavy textures.
- `ritual`: repetitive, meditative, pulse-based, mantra-like.
- `credits`: closing-track energy, reflective resolution.

## Echo feature hints

Infer features from language, but keep uncertainty visible:

- Weather words often shape texture: rain = reverb/grain; storm = tension; sunrise = hope.
- Body words often shape energy: chest, shaking, running, floating, frozen.
- Time words shape palette: midnight = nocturnal; childhood = nostalgia; future = hope/awe.
- Relationship words shape socialness: we/us/their/name references increase relational scoring.
- Repetition and short fragmented entries suggest looping, numbness, or intensity.
