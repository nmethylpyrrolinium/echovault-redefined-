# Wrapped music logic

## Summary objects

Create Wrapped outputs from aggregated emotional listening and echo patterns:

- `soundprintSeason`: the user's dominant emotional season.
- `topMoodFamilies`: ranked mood families by frequency and intensity.
- `recurringSonicMotifs`: repeated textures such as rain piano, neon synth, warm acoustic, heavy drums.
- `emotionalArc`: start/middle/end movement across the time period.
- `creditsTrack`: one representative closing song/category.
- `albumCoverPrompt`: a visual prompt for the period.

## Narrative rules

- Use cinematic language, but keep claims grounded in observable EchoVault signals.
- Avoid diagnosing. Say "your echoes leaned toward..." rather than "you were depressed."
- Make growth visible: name emotional movement, not just top moods.
- Include contradictions when meaningful: "gold on the surface, blue underneath."

## Example Wrapped sections

- "Your year sounded like..."
- "Your most replayed emotional weather"
- "The song category that kept finding you"
- "Your end-credits mood"
- "The orb color of your soundtrack"
