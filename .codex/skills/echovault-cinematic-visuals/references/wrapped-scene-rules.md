# Wrapped Scene Rules

Wrapped scenes can be more cinematic than the core app, but must still feel premium and personal.

## Scene structure

1. Establish atmosphere: gradient, particles, subtle camera depth.
2. Reveal emotional anchor: orb, memory card, stat, or timeline object.
3. Connect meaning: constellation lines, mood weather, or spatial grouping.
4. Resolve: quiet glow, summary text, or shareable composition.

## 3D guidance

- Use 3D to create depth and ceremony, not novelty.
- Avoid fast camera spins and excessive parallax.
- Prefer slow dolly, orbit under 8 degrees, and layered cards at restrained z-depths.
- Always provide a non-3D reduced-motion/static equivalent.

## Export/share surfaces

- Ensure the final scene can pause at a beautiful frame.
- Avoid relying on continuous motion to communicate key information.
