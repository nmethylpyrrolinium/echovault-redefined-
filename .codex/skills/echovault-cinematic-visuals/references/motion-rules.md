# EchoVault Motion Rules

## Timing

- Micro-interactions: 140-240ms.
- Standard page/section transitions: 320-520ms.
- Premium reveal transitions: 560-720ms.
- Wrapped ceremonial beats: 900-1400ms only when staged and skippable.

## Easing

- Default premium ease: `cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)`.
- Calm fade: `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`.
- Avoid springy bounce unless explicitly tied to delight; EchoVault usually favors reverence.

## Emotional movement

- Calm/peaceful: slow drift, wide curves, low amplitude.
- Joyful: brighter pulse, slightly faster twinkle, upward drift.
- Sad/reflective: slow downward or inward drift, lower particle density.
- Angry/intense: tighter orbit, warmer glow, low-frequency shimmer; avoid jitter.
- Hopeful: rising particles, soft gold/cyan accents, staged brightening.
